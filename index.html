Current Projects

Dracula

Dracula is a music music video which will be written and directed by me! The music video is set in the modern day and follows a young reincarnation of Dracula who's lost his memories of being so, and his ring of vampires combine to hunt him down after a night of clubbing to resurrect him through this new vessel.

This music video is not for profit, and is purely for artistic expression. This is a passion project I've been wanting to create for a bit. We are currently in pre-production. The money donated would fund crew transportation, securing locations, purchasing props and decorations, providing meals to the cast and crew, and other miscellaneous expenses. Any and all donations would be greatly appreciated!
